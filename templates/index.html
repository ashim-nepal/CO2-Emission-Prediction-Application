CO2 Emissions Prediction Inputs in Short

Provided following are the details to predict the CO2 emissions:
  • Make: The manufacturer of the vehicle. Example: Audi, BMW, Honda

  • Model: The specific model of the vehicle. Example: A3, Sedan, Odyssey

  • Vehicle Class: The category of the vehicle. Example: Compact, Mid-sized, Minivan

  • Engine Size(L): The engine size in liters. Example: 2.0, 3.5, 5.8

  • Cylinders: The number of cylinders in the engine. Example: 4, 6, 8

  • Transmission: The type of transmission. Example: AS5, AS8, AM7

  • Fuel Type: The type of fuel used. Example: Z, D, X, E, N

  • Fuel Consumption City (L/100 km): Fuel consumption in the city. Example: 8.5, 12.3

  • Fuel Consumption Hwy (L/100 km): Fuel consumption on the highway. Example: 6.2, 9.8

  • Fuel Consumption Comb (L/100 km): Combined fuel consumption. Example: 7.3, 10.5
